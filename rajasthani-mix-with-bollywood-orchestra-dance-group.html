Rajasthani Mix with Bollywood Orchestra Dance Project

Akhepura folk music Project is a top rated Rajasthani band that has all types of performers in its team, from Rajasthani folk dancers to Sufi folk singers to celebrity Rajasthani singers. Our Project of performers takes the audience into a spectacular journey by merging Rajasthani folk music & dance with present famous songs of Bollywood. They mix the powerful rhythms of modern-day instruments together with the melodic chords of instruments used in Rajasthani folk music & dance. If you are looking to add a unique flavor to your event, contact us and see how our performers create an extraordinary and unforgettable experience for your audience.
